Product Testing, Demonstrations, and Literature Surveys
The Synopsys Championship accepts both science and engineering projects at all grade levels. Product Testing type projects (where students test and compare similar items using measurable endpoints) are accepted in grades 6–8 only. Demonstration projects (where students explain how something works) and Literature Surveys are not accepted at any grade level.
